How to Date 101

A bold modern guide for Michal

Step 1
Stop counting down. Obsessing over “the one” rarely works. Relax and maybe play a video game.
Step 2
Pretend you're normal. Laugh at social cues, shower occasionally, and act like a human being.
Step 3
Master messaging. No “hey” messages allowed. Keep it witty, short, and slightly mysterious.
Step 4
Go outside. Parks, cafes, bookstores — anywhere humans exist. Fresh air counts as social interaction.
Step 5
Embrace awkwardness. Smile, laugh, and keep going. Every cringe is a step closer to love.
Step 6
Celebrate mini wins. Got a number, a like, a smile back? Progress is progress, even if slow.
“Dating is weird. Embrace it and have fun!”
